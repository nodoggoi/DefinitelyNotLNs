One day, a beautiful girl like a story - Charlotte Bennett - came to study
abroad in Akito's class. He is attracted to her elegant appearance and cute
appearance that everyone is fascinated by, but Akito gives up even approaching
her because she is out of reach. However, after saving Charlotte's sister, who
was lost, the Bennett sisters began to come to Akito's room every day. As they
spend more time together and become attracted to each other, they eventually
lead a sweet, jittery, sugary life.
